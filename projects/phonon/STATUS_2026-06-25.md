## Key facts to not re-derive

- φ (the LLLA latent) = pooled scalar part of the **last gated block** (`layers[-2]`),
  irreps `32x0e+32x1o+32x1e` → **32-dim**. Tap point marked on the Model diagram.
- The model's last three ops (ReLU · mean-pool · ÷max) are **weightless** — no head to
  be Bayesian over at the very end; that's why mk2 fits its own Linear(32→51) on φ.
- The model **mutates `data.x`/`data.z` in place** — clone the batch for repeated forwards.
- Targets `phdos` are ~peak-normalized; recover absolute scale via ∫g dω = 3N.
- HMC here is a sanity check, not a discovery — the linear-Gaussian head makes Laplace
  exact, so they *must* agree. It earns its keep only if the head goes non-linear later.
- Attribution currently *runs* but isn't *meaningful* until the real-model bundle exists
  (1-epoch φ barely encodes element identity).
